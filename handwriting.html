From Text Intelligence to Script Intelligence

Research-Grade AI for Preserving Arabic Handwriting

Sedrah.ai is expanding from heritage text intelligence to real-time script intelligence, building AI systems that preserve not only what Arabic means, but how it is written.

Language is meaning. Script is embodiment. Sedrah.ai preserves both.

Heritage Language Intelligence

Our work started in advanced NLP with GPU-accelerated pipelines, context-preserving language modeling, and layered emotional transition detection for sacred and historical texts.

  • Precision analysis for heritage corpora
  • Nuance-preserving contextual modeling
  • Structural integrity over naive classification

The Next Frontier: Script Intelligence

We are extending this approach to computational handwriting intelligence that preserves canonical Arabic script geometry in live writing environments.

  • Naskh proportional geometry
  • Baseline and letter-form discipline
  • Diacritic anchoring and stroke sequencing

Core Technical System

  • Real-time Apple Pencil stroke capture (x, y, t, pressure, tilt)
  • Canonical Naskh geometric model with tolerance bands
  • Stroke alignment and deviation detection
  • Immediate adaptive feedback and correction hints
  • Offline-first architecture with optional cloud analytics

Educational and Strategic Impact

  • Classroom dashboards and error-pattern analytics
  • Preservation of script standards and cultural continuity
  • Cross-script extension potential (Urdu, Persian variants)
  • Alignment with GCC education modernization and language preservation initiatives
